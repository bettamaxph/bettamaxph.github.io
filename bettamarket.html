Tank Setups

Php 1000 and up

Aquatic Plants

Php 5 - 1500+

There are varieties of aquatic plants that can be placed in your tank. Avoid those that can damage the fins and block the water surface. They sell 3-5 stems for a cheap price. There are local farms that import foreign aquatic plants, raising demand and selling prices.
