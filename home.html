Betta Keeping

Maintenance of bettas require special considerations to specific practices in terms of husbandry, feeding, inspection and curing of diseases. Variations of maintenance approach varies for each type which makes a differentiation from the common bettas and the others. However, it only takes a small effort to take care of the abovementioned if every step taken is seasoned with love.

TANK SETUP

Before choosing your healthy betta partner, tank preparation is the first priority. The fish will be healthy when its in a suitable and balanced environment. 

BETTA FOOD

A well-nourished betta will be less susceptible of being ill, more vibrant colors and energetic behaviour. It is also important in grooming your betta since what it eats is what promotes its growth.

Common Betta 

This kind of betta developed throughout the years for pet and show purposes. It is also readily available in pet shops since it is common. It also has more varieties like veiltails, crown tails, halfmoon and such. Common bettas have many names due to their forms and colors.

Alien Betta

This kind is uncommon in pet trade since it is mainly acquired in the wild and some are hard to breed and spawn in captivity.

BETTA FISH FIGHT

Mainly, engaging in betta fish fights may lead to damage or even death to the fishes. Bettas are innately territorial, and due to constant breeding, there comes an increased aggression genes that affect their behavior. Bettas show dominance by expanding their fins and gills, an intimidation display, to make them look larger than their normal size. Betta fish fights, especially those who are still beginners for this activity, should be regulated and be knowledgable about the risks and limits for the abovementioned.
